Nestled in the heart of Ahangama, The Horizon Breeze is your ultimate escape to coastal bliss. Our hotel combines modern comfort with the charm of tropical serenity, offering guests an unforgettable experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions about The Horizon Breeze . From booking details to amenities and local attractions, we've got you covered.

1. What amenities are included in the rooms?

All our rooms include A/C, hot water, free WiFi, and comfortable bedding. Selected rooms offer beautiful garden or lake views.

2. How can I book a room or villa?

You can book directly through our website or contact us for assistance with reservations.

3. Is breakfast included in the stay?

Please check with our team as breakfast options may vary based on the booking package.

4. Is airport transportation available?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers upon request. Please contact us in advance for details.

5. Is there a spa available?

Yes, our Herbal SPA offers relaxing treatments using natural ingredients for a rejuvenating experience.

6. Do you offer yoga sessions?

Yes, we provide guided yoga sessions in a serene setting for relaxation and wellness.
